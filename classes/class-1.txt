Class: Oven

Attributes:
ovenOn (boolean)
clean (boolean)
currentTemperture (integer)
settings (array)
color (string)

Methods:
startOven (changes ovenOn attribute to TRUE)
cleanOven (switches clean attribute from FALSE to TRUE)
preheatOven (changes currentTemperture attribute from 0 to 350)
ovenBake (changes settings attribute to bake)
